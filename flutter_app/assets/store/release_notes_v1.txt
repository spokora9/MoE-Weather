Version 1.0.0 — Initial Release

Welcome to MoE Weather! We're thrilled to put hyper-local, multi-model forecasts in your hands for the very first time.

What's included in this release:

• Multi-model hourly forecasts up to 48 hours and a full 7-day outlook
• Air quality index with EPA health categories
• Astronomy data including sunrise, sunset, and moon phase
• Marine and coastal conditions with NOAA tide predictions
• Severe weather alerts from the National Weather Service delivered in real time

We'd love to hear what you think. Tap the feedback button in Settings or leave us a review — every comment helps us make MoE Weather better for everyone. Thank you for being here on day one.
