## Full Description

### MoE Weather — Multi-Model Local Forecasts

MoE Weather delivers hyper-local, multi-model weather forecasts so you always know what's coming — hour by hour, day by day, and along the coast.

Most weather apps rely on a single forecast model. MoE Weather is different. It aggregates data from multiple authoritative sources — including NOAA's National Weather Service, GFS, ECMWF, and regional mesoscale models — then presents the consensus forecast alongside model disagreement so you can judge the confidence level for yourself. Whether you're planning a weekend hike, a beach day, or just deciding what to wear, MoE Weather gives you the full picture.

### Key Features

- **Hourly forecasts up to 48 hours** — temperature, precipitation chance, wind speed & direction, and feels-like at a glance
- **7-day extended forecast** — daily high/low, precipitation summary, and dominant conditions
- **Air quality index with EPA categories** — know when the air is unhealthy before you head outside
- **Astronomy data** — precise sunrise and sunset times, moon phase, moonrise and moonset for every day
- **Marine and coastal conditions** — wave height, swell period, water temperature, and tide predictions powered by NOAA CO-OPS
- **Severe weather alerts** — NWS watches, warnings, and advisories pushed directly to your device the moment they're issued
- **Home screen widgets (Pro)** — glanceable current conditions and the next 6 hours without opening the app
- **Push notifications for alerts and daily briefings (Pro)** — morning and evening summaries delivered on your schedule
- **Multiple saved locations (Pro)** — manage weather for home, work, family, and favorite destinations all in one place
- **Ad-free experience (Pro)** — no banners, no interstitials, no distractions
